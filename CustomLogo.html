Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a premium logo and a logo template?

A premium logo is customized according to your business needs, and it is unique to your brand only. With a premium custom logo design, you own the logo file, and nobody else has access to it. If you want, you can also file for copyrights and trademark to secure your exceptional logo design for as long as your business exists.

A logo template on the other hand is a design template that can be customized by many users using a logo maker, and they can download it as many times. The design is copyrighted by the logo creator company.
