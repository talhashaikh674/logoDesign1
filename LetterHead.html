Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to include my logo in the letterhead template?

No, you don’t need to include your logo but it is advisable. Branding starts with a Logo Design and it is good practice to brand your letterhead with a logo to create a better impression on your audience.

However, if you are looking to create a resume or simple document then including contact information related to your business is enough.

What kind of information do I include in my letterhead design?

It is customary to include your business contact information on letterheads. Just like information on your Business Card Design help people find and connect with you, letterhead information can include company name or brand logo, street address, and phone number.

In more elaborate letterhead designs, you could include email, website, job title, or perhaps even your picture.

Does your letterhead maker allow editing to fill my information?

Of course. Our letterhead maker has placeholders where you can easily edit to add your own contact information as you require. If you don’t want to add more information, you can delete the redundant information fields too.
