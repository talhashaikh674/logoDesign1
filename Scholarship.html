Become a Featured Logo Designer and Win USD $500 Scholarship

LogoDesign.net is awarding a USD $500 (or equivalent local currency) scholarship every month to the student who submits the most popular design. Not only will you win a USD $500 scholarship but you will also become a featured logo designer on our website. You will be seen by thousands of graphic designers as well as companies looking for graphic designers. It’s a great opportunity to be hired after you graduate from school.

We understand being a student can be financially tough. This is why we are offering this scholarship to provide graphic design students with a way to earn income while being able to study and improve their learned skills.

The Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an institute or school studying graphic design
  • Maintains a minimum GPA of 2.5
  • Submit logos in editable files such as .AI or .EPS together with .PNG.
  • Design one (1) original logo design concept for the theme of the month.
  • Upload your design as a graphic file of 800x800 pixels, on a transparent background.

The Award

  • A USD $500 scholarship amount (or equivalent local currency)
  • The opportunity to be featured on LogoDesign.net website and be seen by thousands of people

The Award

To apply, please follow the steps below.

  • Step 1: Design a logo based on the theme for the month. You will find more details about this below.
  • Step 2: Submit your logo concepts in AI, EPS and PNG formats along with details about your education, school/college/university, and location.
  • Step 3: Once you submit your logo, a panel of expert graphic designers will select and give ratings to your logo along with designs submitted by other participants.
  • Step 4: A winner is selected for the scholarship contest and amount is awarded!

*Do not forget to check out the theme of the month before starting.
